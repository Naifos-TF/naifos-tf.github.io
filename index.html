GONE ROGUE

UNREAL ENGINE

This game was made in a week for the Brackey's Gamejam 2022 with my sister.

We aimed for a fast paced FPS. Since the player is playing as an AI, we gave the whole game a futuristic tone (weapon, font, dialogues, sounds). Also, to have a short and fun experience in a gamejam context, we chose to set the game to last for 5 minutes maximum.


My contribution

  • Designed the 4 powers (healing zone, deploy turret, freeze time, call an ally) that the player can use to survive
  • Tuned all the stats for characters, enemies, powers etc
  • Did the level design
  • Implemented features with blueprints (countdown, health/death system, healing and time freeze powers, audio, dialogues and UI...)

Challenges

  • Creating a coherent overall environment without a profesional artist in our team
  • Reducing the scope and scraping features to complete the jam in time
  • Balancing the overall difficulty for a 5 min experience

PURRFECT RUN

UNITY

This game was made over the course of 2 weeks as a project to enhance my skills in mobile game development and to publish an app on the Google Play Store.


I aimed for a hypercasual 3D runner where the player controls a cat on an endless run. The main objective is to collect as many fishes as possible while avoiding obstacles. The game accelerates the more fishes the player collects, increasing the challenge and excitement. Additionally, Unity Ads are integrated to monetize the game effectively.


My Contribution

  • Developed the infinite run mechanics, ensuring smooth and continuous gameplay.
  • Implemented the object collection system, allowing the cat to collect fishes seamlessly.
  • Programmed the acceleration mechanics, increasing the game’s speed as the player collects more fishes.
  • Integrated Unity Ads to display advertisements, providing a source of revenue for the game.
  • Created a simple tutorial to guide new players through the game mechanics.

Challenges

  • Balancing the game's difficulty to keep it engaging yet challenging as the speed increases.
  • Ensuring smooth performance on a variety of mobile devices, optimizing both gameplay and ads integration.
  • Designing an intuitive and appealing UI/UX to enhance the player’s experience.

QUICK ! DICE IT !

UNITY

This game was made in less than 48 hours for the GMTK 2022 Game Jam, based on the theme "Roll Of The Dice".


Our vision was to create a game inspired by Wario Ware, where the player engages in a series of crazy mini-games. Each mini-game presents a unique challenge, reflecting the randomness and excitement of rolling dice.


My Contribution

  • Defined the game vision, mechanics, and tuned the variables to ensure balanced gameplay.
  • Implemented several mini-games using Unity C# scripts, ensuring they were fun and challenging.
  • Created 2D assets with Aseprite, contributing to the visual appeal and consistency of the game.

Challenges

  • Designing different very short mini-games that could be implemented and tested within a tight 48-hour timeframe.
  • Ensuring each mini-game was intuitive yet varied, providing a fresh experience for the player.
  • Balancing the difficulty and randomness to keep the gameplay engaging and fair.

Blastoise AR

Main AR concepts : AR marker, XR Reference image, Spatial audio, AR UX/UI

The app features intuitive controls that make it easy to rotate the 3D model and play the Pokemon's sound. Users can also view detailed information about its abilities, weaknesses, and evolution chain.

VR Work

VR - Shooting Range

I created a basic VR shooting range prototype in Unity, featuring fundamental shooting mechanics and simple graphics, designed to offer a straightforward and functional experience for users new to virtual reality.

This early-stage project focuses on core functionalities, setting the foundation for future enhancements and more detailed immersive experiences.

VR - Throw The Ball

In another one of my VR prototypes, I developed a simple yet engaging game where players pick up colored balls and aim to throw them at matching colored targets.

This project, created in Unity, focuses on fundamental VR interactions, emphasizing hand-eye coordination and color recognition in a straightforward, user-friendly virtual environment.

VR - Lightsaber

I made a basic Star Wars-inspired game using Unity, where players can experiment with a lightsaber to interact with troopers in a simple environment.

The prototype offers basic mechanics like lightsaber combat and deflecting laser shots, serving as a foundational step towards more intricate VR experiences in the realm of sci-fi adventure.

VR - Room

In my interactive VR project, players find themselves in a virtual room where they can engage with everyday objects, such as using a remote to turn on a TV or playfully handling a tennis ball and racket, demonstrating the potential of VR in simulating real-world interactions.

This immersive experience showcases the seamless integration of common physical activities into a virtual environment, offering a glimpse into the practical applications of VR technology.
